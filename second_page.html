A POEM
I, D-503, the builder of the Integral, I am only one of the many mathematicians of the United State.
The great historic hour is near, when the first Interal will rise into the limitless space of the universe. A thousand yers ago your heroic ancestors subjected the whole earth to the power of the United State. A still more glorious task is before you, the integration of the indefinite equation of the Cosmos by the use of glass electric fire.
